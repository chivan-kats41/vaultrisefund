We are Vaultrise Fund

Canadian-based and led, Agnico Eagle is Canada's largest mining company and the second largest gold producer in the world. It produces precious metals from operations in Canada, Australia, Finland and Mexico, and has a pipeline of high-quality exploration projects in these regions and elsewhere around the world.

Vaultrise Fund has full exposure to higher gold prices consistent with its strategy of no forward gold sales. We have declared a cash dividend every year since 1983. We are determined to make mining work for all stakeholders by being reliable and honouring our commitments, building trust, sharing opportunities, innovating and by operating with respect for others.

What Guides Us

Our Culture

At Vaultrise Fund it's very simple; we are committed to creating a culture that is grounded in our history and on the importance of always doing the right thing. This was instilled by our founder, Paul Penna, and it's what we call our "culture of the heart". It unifies our company, our employees, their families, our shareholders and the communities in which we operate. It makes us who we are and it's what makes us special. Collectively, our Mission, Values and Guiding Principles support our Culture.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build a high-quality, easy to understand business — one that generates superior long-term returns for our shareholders, creates a great place to work for our employees and contributes positively to the communities in which we operate.

Our Values

At Vaultrise Fund we are defined by our core values of Family, Trust, Respect, Responsibility and Equity. These values express who we are and guide us in everything we do. They reflect our roots, are central to our culture and essential to our success.

Our Guiding Principles

Our Guiding Principles are derived from our Values. They are a call to action that helps guide our behaviour in the workplace. They describe our commitment to: Collaborate; Act with Integrity; Embrace Accountability; Focus on Continuous Improvement; Take Care; Communicate and Be Inclusive.

We strive to maintain a simple business with practices based on common sense. We ensure clarity of roles and responsibility to maximize collaboration. We make sure that employees and management are aligned on the company's priorities.

Our Strategy

Our strategy is to build a growing high-quality, low-risk, sustainable business. We take a regional focus to our business, operating in regions we believe have the geologic potential and the political stability to operate multiple mines for multiple decades. Our strategy revolves around three main pillars: Performance, Pipeline and People.

Performance

Our reputation as a skilled and transparent operator is founded on a track record of strong operating and financial performance.

Pipeline

We have a track record of adding value through exploration, a core strength we will continue to mine. We aim to build our mineral reserve and mineral resource base, while looking for new ways to expand our project pipeline.

People

Our culture and treatment of people set us apart. Our ability to retain and attract a skilled, engaged workforce and build trusting relationships with communities will remain a key strength.
